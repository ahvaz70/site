Directions to Horse Country Club

Step by Step Directions from Ibiza

From Ibiza take the PM804 towards Santa Gertrudis.

After about 11km you will go past the BP petrol station on the right; this is about half a kilometer before you reach Santa Gertrudis.

Entering Santa Gertrudis you will come to a roundabout.
Go straight across.

Stay on the PM804 towards Sant Miquel.

Go past the Pharmacy on the left.

Turn left at the next roundabout

Turn IMMEDIATELY right.
Drive away from Santa Gertrudis.

Keep going straight at this junction.

Follow the road for about four kilometres.

Almost there.

Watch the left of the road for the Horse Country Club sign.
This marks the turn into the camino for the stables.

Turn left at the sign.
Follow the Camino for 0.5 km
You will see the stables on your right hand side

Please drive in slowly

Enjoy your ride.
